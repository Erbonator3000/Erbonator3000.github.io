Junction hackathon entry: HeadCase concussion detection

2017

HeadCase is a mobile application which warns the user of possible concussions. The app pairs with Suunto movesense motion sensor which is attached to the users head. In the case of powerful impact, the app fires a notification. The sensor could be worn for example by a kid doing sports and the app could be used by the parent. Alternatively, the sensor could be attached to a construction workers helmet and the app could automatically alert the first aid personnel in case of an accident.
