Nicotine pouch can battery holder

2023

After legalisation of nicotine pouch sales in Finland, the plastic waste from these cans exploded. As an electronics enthusiast I designed a simple 3D printable battery holder to accommodate these trash in to handy project boxes.

The design is a simple, fast printing insert for the bottom compartment of the pouch can, which can hold two AA batteries. Small sensor board or similar can be then situated in the top compartment.
